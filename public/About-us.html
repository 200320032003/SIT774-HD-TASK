About Us

Who Are We?

OtaZora started as a late-night idea in my bedroom, fueled by way too much anime, instant noodles, and the dream of finding more people who just “get it.” My name’s Oshadhi, and yep, it’s just me running the show for now! I created OtaZora because I always wanted a place where fans could connect, swap merch, share recommendations, and feel like they belong. No matter if you’re a total newbie or an absolute Otaku legend.

What started as a solo project has grown into a friendly little corner of the internet for anime fans in Australia and (hopefully) beyond. From the latest merchandise drops to news, local events, and online meetups, I’m working hard to bring you a space that’s fun, welcoming, and totally dedicated to the anime universe. I believe anime is more than just entertainment; it’s creativity, friendship, and culture all rolled into one.

If you’re reading this, thanks for stopping by! Whether you want to join an event, geek out over a new series, or just find your next anime buddy, OtaZora is here for you. And who knows? Maybe one day, this “team” page will have more faces. For now, it’s just one fan with a big dream and a lot of code. Welcome to the family!
